Each link below will take you to a page that shows the official issue page from both Hillary Clinton and Bernie Sanders for the given issue side by side. The only modification we made to these pages is the color of the text of each sentence. We used a machine learning algorithm to determine which sentences on these pages do a better job of discriminating between candidates. Darker sentences do a better job according to the algorithm, whereas lighter sentences do not. The idea is that if someone wants to quickly scross through these pages and quickly determine what types of text makes each candidate unique, they can skip over less informative sentences.
